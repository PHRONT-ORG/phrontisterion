PHRONTISTERION
Inquiry (private)

What this is

This is a private inquiry channel for adults who want to request an explanation of a parable or ask about private formation.

Boundaries

Adults (18+) only. Participation is voluntary and you may exit at any time. This project does not provide medical, legal, or mental-health advice.

How to inquire

Use the inquiry form on the Parables page.

If you do not receive a reply, assume the inquiry was declined or did not pass safeguards.
